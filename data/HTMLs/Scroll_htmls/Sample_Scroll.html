This is a mysterious scroll with a note inside...



The note reads...


A survivors note...


Well I am leaving this town, I have lived here in Balota for over 40 years and this virus has taken over.


I have lost my entire family and I have nothing left here in Balota to stay around for anymore.


If anyone finds this I am heading North West of here to a close by town called Zelenogorsk, meet me in the church.
